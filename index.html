Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on advancing technology and creative solutions to drive economic growth.

Sustainability

It prioritizes renewable energy and eco-friendly practices for a greener future.

Infrastructure

The plan emphasizes building modern facilities to improve connectivity and global competitiveness.

Empowerment

It aims to provide equal opportunities and resources to uplift every community.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
